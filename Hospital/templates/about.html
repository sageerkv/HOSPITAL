    About Us

    CITY CARE is a large size of hospital, located in periyaarpuram. We are proud to serve our community and have been providing high-quality care to patients for 10 years. Our hospital has 500 beds and 8 departments, providing a wide range of medical services to our patients. We are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed with highly skilled healthcare professionals who are committed to providing compassionate and personalized care to each patient. Each year, we serve 2500 patients, making us a leading healthcare provider in our region. We are dedicated to meeting the healthcare needs of our community and strive to provide the highest level of care to all who seek our services. Overall, our hospital is an integral part of the community, providing essential healthcare services to patients in need. We are committed to improving the health and well-being of our community through "our mission of provide exceptional, patient-centered healthcare services to our community. We are committed to delivering the highest quality care with compassion, respect, and integrity. Our goal is to promote health and wellness, prevent illness and injury, and provide a safe and welcoming environment for all who seek our services. We strive to be a trusted partner in our patients' health journeys, and we are dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve."
